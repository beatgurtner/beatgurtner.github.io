The Concept

💪 ➡️ 🎵 ➡️ 😌 ➡️ 🎶

Your power meter tells the app when you're working hard (above threshold). Pumping music plays automatically.

When you ease off for recovery (below threshold), the app switches to chill, relaxing music.

No manual controls needed. Just ride. The music adapts to your effort.

⚠️ Power meter required! The app needs real-time wattage data to detect your work/recovery phases.
